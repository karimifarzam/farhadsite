Mission

Our mission is to empower blockchain with artificial intelligence to improve transactions, support alternatives to traditional financial services, and create the blockchain infrastructure for a post Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) world; supporting UBI, on-chain voting, humanity verification, and whatever else the future requires.

What is Form?

FormAI is the AI agent infrastructure for the blockchain. The FormAI network acts as the execution layer for AI on-chain. Our lab develops open-source transactional agents and provides the agentic infrastructure for developers to easily create and host powerful decentralized AI applications.

Team

Our team is composed of accomplished researchers and developers affiliated with prominent institutions such as Harvard University, MIT, The Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence, and the University of Waterloo.
